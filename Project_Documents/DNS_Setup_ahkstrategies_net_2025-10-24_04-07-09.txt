TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

If domain doesn't work after 24 hours:

1. Verify DNS records at: https://dnschecker.org
   - Enter: ahkstrategies.net
   - Check A record shows: 76.76.21.21

2. Check Vercel domain status:
   vercel domains inspect ahkstrategies.net

3. Look for common issues:
   ☐ Multiple A records for root domain (keep only one)
   ☐ Wrong IP address (must be 76.76.21.21)
   ☐ TTL too high (use 3600 or 14400)
   ☐ DNS cache (try different network/device)

4. Clear DNS cache on your computer:
   Windows: ipconfig /flushdns
   Mac: sudo dscacheutil -flushcache

5. Contact Vercel support if issues persist
