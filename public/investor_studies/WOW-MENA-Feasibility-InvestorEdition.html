Critical Risk Factors

‚ö†Ô∏è Foreign Exchange Risk

The business model incurs COGS in Euros (CKD kits from Italy) while generating revenues in Egyptian Pounds. Egypt's recent 38% inflation peak and currency volatility create direct margin compression risk.

Mitigation: Built-in 10% COGS contingency, phased localization roadmap (Phase 2-3), hedging strategy for large orders.

‚ö†Ô∏è Policy Dependency

Egypt's EGP 50,000 subsidy (~‚Ç¨993) for locally-produced EVs is a cornerstone of competitive pricing. Any government policy shift would fundamentally alter project economics.

Impact: 25% subsidy reduction drops IRR from 18.2% to 15.8%. Complete subsidy elimination would require immediate price repositioning or market exit.

Mitigation: Proactive government relations, diversified revenue (UAE premium market), accelerated localization to maximize subsidy eligibility duration.

üîß Localization Roadmap

Phase 1 (Year 0-1): CKD Assembly ‚Äì Import complete kits, assemble locally to qualify for base incentives.

Phase 2 (Year 2-3): Component Sourcing ‚Äì Local plastic moldings, wiring harnesses, suspension. Target 40% local content.

Phase 3 (Year 4-5): Strategic Manufacturing ‚Äì Battery pack assembly partnership, motor controller localization. Target 60%+ local content.

Financial Impact: 2-3 percentage point gross margin improvement in Y4-Y5 as CKD import dependency reduces.
